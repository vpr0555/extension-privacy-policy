Privacy Policy for Chrome Extensions

This Privacy Policy applies to the Google Chrome Extensions (the "Extension") developed by this developer that link to this page.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We take your privacy seriously.

The Extension is designed to perform its functions locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or identifiable data to any external servers or third parties.

2. Permissions

The Extension only requests the minimum permissions necessary to provide its features. Depending on the specific extension you are using, it may request one or more of the following permissions:

  • contextMenus: To add useful options to the browser's right-click menu.
  • scripting / activeTab: To interact with the current web page content to perform the extension's core features (e.g., finding text or links).
  • storage: To save your user preferences and settings locally within your browser.
  • Host Permissions: To allow the extension to function on the websites you visit, triggered only by user interaction.
  • clipboardWrite: To copy requested information to your clipboard.

These permissions are used only to execute the functionality described in the extension's store page and never for data harvesting or monitoring.

3. Third-Party Services

If an Extension interacts with a third-party service (such as Google Search or a translation engine), your interaction is subject to that provider's privacy policy. We do not receive or store any data from these interactions.

4. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in our extensions or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.
